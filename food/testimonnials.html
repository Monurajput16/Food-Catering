DAN L.

Jeff and his crew did a fantastic job at our wedding last Saturday!

He brought the vision for our dinner, Southern Style, to life with barbequed short ribs, buttermilk roasted chicken, macaroni and cheese, Brussel sprouts, coleslaw, and cornbread/biscuits. He was very attentive when we wanted things tweaked and executed them to perfection. The family style serving ensured that everyone had enough of exactly what they wanted and our guests loved it. We had several, SEVERAL, of our guests come up to us to not only compliment the food, but the family style, and even the shabby chic plates (nearly everyone has a different plate). The shabby chic is part of what sold us. At our first tasting and this was totally random, I was eating off of my grandparents plates, while my now wife was eating off of plates she grew up with.

We even stole Jeff's music from our tastings for our wedding dinner. We're big country music fans and he had it on during each and every tasting and we just loved the atmosphere.

We may be biased, but we both agreed we've never been to a wedding with better food! Our only wish was that we had more of an appetite the night of. Jeff and his crew even made sure we had appetizers at our table since we missed most of them while we were mingling during our cocktail hour.

About us

We pride ourselves on creating unique menus to
accommodate every special event. From casual in-home
brunches and office lunches to elegant weddings and
glamorous film/tv productions.

Of FOOD is a subsidiary of Dream Foods Inc.

SERVICE

Our catering services are perfect for any size, any style, and are customized to your budget.

WEDDING

We created two wedding packages to make planning your wedding easier than ever. We guide you through curating the perfect menu for you and your guests.

CORPORATE

Our corporate lunch catering package is an easy drop-off or pick-up service. Simply contact us and like magic, large trays of restaurant-quality food will come to you.

SOCIALS

From birthday or anniversary parties to non-profit fundraisers and more, our social catering menu will have you breaking bread with your closest friends and community partners.

HOLIDAYS

Our holiday catering menus are sure to impress. Let our team handle the cooking while you sit back and entertain your friends and family
